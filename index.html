    Re-Design / Refresh

    Is your current logo feeling outdated or struggling to adapt to the new digital landscape? Do you need a secondary logo or a different format? Our logo redesign and refresh services start at $80+, depending on your specific revisions and budget. Whether you're a small startup or an established business, my focus will be dedicated to listening your concerns and challenges and finding the correct solution.

    What's the difference between Redesign and refresh?


    A logo refresh involves making subtle updates to an existing logo to modernize or refine its appearance while maintaining its core elements. This process might include adjusting colors, fonts, or minor graphic elements to ensure the logo remains relevant and visually appealing in the current market. A refresh is ideal for brands looking to stay contemporary without losing their established identity and recognition.


    On the other hand, a logo redesign is a comprehensive overhaul of a brand's visual identity, often starting from scratch. This process involves creating an entirely new logo that may significantly differ from the original in terms of design, concept, and symbolism. A redesign is typically pursued when a company undergoes significant changes, such as rebranding, mergers, or shifts in business direction, requiring a fresh and distinct representation of its new identity and goals.
