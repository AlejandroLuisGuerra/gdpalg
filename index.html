Website Creation

Your website is often the first interaction customers have with your brand, and it is crucial to make that experience outstanding. Step one will be a comprehensive discovery phase, where I learn about your goals, audience, and unique selling points. I then combine cutting-edge design principles with the latest technology to develop a site that is not only visually stunning but also user-friendly and optimized for performance. Throughout the design process, open communication is encouraged, welcoming your input to ensure the final product aligns with your vision. My dedication to excellence ensures that every detail, from layout to functionality, is meticulously crafted to provide an engaging and seamless user experience, ultimately driving your business success online.

Tier 1: $500

Includes a professionally designed single-page website, perfect for personal portfolios or small businesses needing a concise online presence. This package covers basic SEO, responsive design, and a user-friendly interface.

Tier 2: $1200

This tier is ideal for clients looking for a more robust online presence, offering up to five custom-designed pages, enhanced with advanced SEO strategies, and integrated with social media and basic e-commerce functionality. This package also includes a thorough UX review to ensure an intuitive user journey.

Tier 3: $2000

For businesses aiming for a comprehensive digital experience, tier 3 provides a fully customized website with up to ten pages, advanced e-commerce capabilities, and a complete UX design process informed by detailed user research and testing. This package features high-quality graphic design, interactive elements, and ongoing support and optimization to ensure your website remains at the cutting edge.

    Logo Design

    I understand that your logo is the visual cornerstone of your brand identity, and this task is approached with meticulous attention to detail. My process begins with an in-depth consultation to truly understand your vision, values, and target audience. Thorough research and brainstorming sessions then occur, ensuring that every concept developed is unique and resonates with your brand essence. Multiple iterations are tested, refining each element until the final design not only meets but exceeds your expectations. I will work towards involving you at every stage to ensure the logo we deliver is an accurate representation of your business, ready to leave a lasting impression in your industry.

    Tier 1: $200 (1-2 weeks)

    Three initial design concepts and two rounds of revisions.
    Final Deliverables:
  • PNG (transparent packground)
  • JPG (high resolution)
  • SVG (vector format)
  • Short document on basic logo usage (colors, size, and placement)

    • Tier 2: $500 (2-4 weeks)

  • PNG (transparent packground)

  • SVG (vector format)
  • Different versions of the logo (color, black and white, and single color)
  • Detailed brand style guide (color codes, typography, and usage rules)
  • Logo versions optimized for social media profiles and headers.

    • Tier 3: $1000 (4-8 weeks)

    Seven initial concepts, unlimited revisions until final approval.
  • PNG (transparent packground)

  • Complete set of logo versions optimized for all major social media platforms
  • Business card, letterhead, and envelope design templates.
