Website Development


  • UX/UI Porfolio

My commitment is to ensure that each website I create is not only visually appealing but also delivers an exceptional user experience, tailored to meet the specific needs and goals of your business. I offer three types of website creation services tailored to fit different budgets and requirements. For those seeking a cost-effective solution, my $500 package includes a professionally designed single-page website, perfect for personal portfolios or small businesses needing a concise online presence. This package covers basic SEO, responsive design, and a user-friendly interface.


The mid-tier $1200 package is ideal for clients looking for a more robust online presence, offering up to five custom-designed pages, enhanced with advanced SEO strategies, and integrated with social media and basic e-commerce functionality. This package also includes a thorough UX review to ensure an intuitive user journey.


For businesses aiming for a comprehensive digital experience, the $2000 premium package provides a fully customized website with up to ten pages, advanced e-commerce capabilities, and a bespoke UX design process informed by detailed user research and testing. This package features high-quality graphic design, interactive elements, and ongoing support and optimization to ensure your website remains at the cutting edge.
